Personal video publishing for connected social accounts.

6Gate is a private dashboard that helps its owner publish videos to their own connected social media destinations, including TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook Pages, using official platform APIs where available.

Read Privacy Policy Read Terms

6Gate is a personal-use tool. It is not a public social network, engagement automation service, scraping tool, or advertising platform.

  • Connect owned social accounts through official OAuth or platform-provided authorization flows.
  • Select a local video file, destination group, caption, title, and privacy setting.
  • Create one upload job per destination and track each job with its own status stream.
  • Cancel, retry, or review upload jobs from the local dashboard.

Built for controlled personal publishing

6Gate focuses on publishing content that the owner manually selects. It does not collect audiences, scrape third-party profiles, or manipulate engagement metrics.

Destination groups

Create groups of owned channels, pages, or accounts and submit one video to multiple destinations.

Official account access

Connected accounts are authorized through supported provider flows and used only for authenticated publishing actions.

Transparent job status

Each destination upload creates a job with logs, status updates, cancellation, and retry controls.

Data use and platform compliance

6Gate uses account identifiers, authorization tokens, video metadata, local file paths, and job logs only to authenticate connected accounts, submit selected videos, and display upload progress. 6Gate does not sell personal data or share data with advertisers or data brokers.

Content published through 6Gate remains subject to the destination platform's own terms, community guidelines, review systems, and moderation decisions.
