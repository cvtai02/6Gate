# Handoff Rules

- Backend-to-UI handoff is required for API, DTO, endpoint, auth, pagination, filtering, sorting, or validation changes.
- UI-to-backend handoff is required for new backend requirements.
- Move completed backend-to-UI handoffs to `handoffs/backend-to-ui/archive/`.
- Move completed UI-to-backend handoffs to `handoffs/ui-to-backend/archive/`.
