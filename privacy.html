Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

6Gate is a personal-use video publishing dashboard used to connect owned social media accounts and publish user-selected videos to selected destinations. This Privacy Policy explains what information 6Gate processes and how it is used.

1. Information 6Gate Processes

Depending on the connected platform and selected features, 6Gate may process:

  • Account identifiers, usernames, display names, profile images, page/channel names, and destination IDs returned by official platform APIs.
  • OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens, page tokens, and related authorization data needed to make authenticated API requests.
  • Video metadata provided by the user, such as title, caption, description, privacy setting, content type, and destination group.
  • Local video file paths or temporary server-side video files needed to execute upload jobs.
  • Upload job records, status values, provider post IDs, provider URLs, logs, retry attempts, cancellation events, and error details.

2. How Information Is Used

6Gate uses the information above only to:

  • Authenticate connected accounts through supported provider authorization flows.
  • Display connected accounts and publishing destinations in the dashboard.
  • Upload and publish videos that the user manually selects.
  • Track upload progress, display job logs, support cancellation, and allow retry of non-successful jobs.
  • Troubleshoot errors and maintain reliable personal publishing workflows.

3. TikTok Data Use

When a TikTok account is connected, 6Gate uses TikTok authorization data only to authenticate API requests and publish videos selected by the user to the connected TikTok account. 6Gate does not scrape TikTok, collect information about other TikTok users, automate engagement, or sell TikTok data.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

6Gate sends information to social platforms only as necessary to authenticate connected accounts, sync owned destinations, and publish selected videos. 6Gate does not sell personal information, share data with advertisers, or provide data to data brokers.

5. Storage and Retention

6Gate is designed as a private/personal-use tool. Data may be stored locally or in the environment where the application is deployed. Authorization tokens are retained only while an account remains connected. Job records and logs may be retained for troubleshooting and auditability. Temporary video files may be retained only as needed to complete upload jobs.

6. User Controls

The user can disconnect accounts, cancel destination upload jobs, retry failed or cancelled jobs, and delete job records where the application provides those controls. To request account or data deletion, contact the project owner.

7. Security

6Gate is intended to be operated by the owner in a controlled environment. The owner is responsible for securing the host, database, environment variables, OAuth credentials, and access to the dashboard.

8. Children

6Gate is not intended for children and is not directed to users under the age required by the connected social platforms.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the application changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support@6gate.nekomin.com.
